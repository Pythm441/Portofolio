🧩 Memory problems: forgetting new info and names.

🧓 Dementia is a general term for a decline in mental ability (memory, language, thinking) severe enough to affect daily life. It is not a normal part of aging ⏳.
echo "Link and difference"
🔗 Alzheimer’s is the main cause of dementia. Alzheimer’s = disease 🧠; dementia = symptom group 🧩.
